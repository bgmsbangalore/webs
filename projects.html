Projects

Kanvashrama

A home for orphans and destitute children, Kanvashram was started in 1975 and more than 2500 children have passed through the Ashram’s portal since then. At present the ashram is home to 230 children aged Six years and above. Children are housed in six dormitories and are provided with all amenities like food, clothing, medical care, education and extra-curricular activities

BGMS Shishukunj Vidhyalaya

BGMS ShishuKunj Vidhyalaya an English medium school from class one to class 10 and was established in 2009 with support from Shishukunj London. The management and staff at the school are fully committed to delivering the best education in a child friendly environment – to allow for holistic and all round development. Volunteers of Bhoomi Foundation are engaged in after school activities for the children.

Anandham

Anand Dham is an old age home built with the help of Rotary Club of Bangalore Indiranagar and Jai Shiv Shakthi Foundation to provide a homely and respectable living for elders with utmost commitment and care, It is a home for neglected destitute elderly women, Widows, Handicapped and Orphans who are aged 60 and above. Inmates are sheltered with sufficient space, homely atmosphere, medical & health care facilities along with nutritious food and clothing.

Parijatha

Parijatha is a college girls hostel was built in 2017 with the help of Rotary club of Bangalore Indiranagar and Jai Shiv Shakthi Foundation for needy girl students who complete 10th standard with good scores and wish to continue their education further.
Accommodation, food, medical care and college fees are provided from BGMS

Santhwana

The scheme was sanctioned by Government of Karnataka under Women and child development department, during the year 2000-01 with an objective to assist women who are victims of domestic violence, rape, sexual abuse and dowry harassment. It aims at providing legal assistance, temporary shelter, financial relief and training to enable them to be self-reliant and also to achieve social and economic empowerment.
We have two help lines managed by highly qualified and trained counselors in operation round the clock to provide guidance, counseling and assistance to women in distress. Santvana centre at BGMS covers the Bangalore rural District, comprising of Nelamangala, Ramanagar, Channapatna, Doddaballapur, Devanahalli, Magadi and Kanakapura Taluq.

Swadhar

The scheme functions under Government of Karnataka under Women and child development department as per the guidlines set by the Government.
A Short Stay home or Swadhar Gruh for women in distress and who are without any social and economic support, who have suffered trauma and abuse at the hands of their husbands/in Laws/others or who have suddenly find themselves without a home, can find immediate shelter in BGMS.
They will be provided with legal aid, guidance and vocational training to enable them to take steps for their readjustment in family/society.
Vocational training includes various trades like tailoring, home nursing, beautician’s course etc. to rehabilitate them economically and emotionally
